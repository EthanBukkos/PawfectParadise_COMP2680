About Us

Founded in 2024, we are one of the Canada's largest and most trusted network of pet care and adoption services. Whether you need in-home pet grooming, boarding, training, or are looking to give a stray pet a forever home, we're here to help you at every step.

Our goal is to ensure that every pet receives the love, care, and family they deserve. From supporting NGOs and rescue shelters to rehoming abandoned pets, we work tirelessly to make a difference.

Our Mission

At Pawfect Paradise, we believe in providing a second chance to animals in need. Our primary focus is on:

  • Rehoming orphaned, abandoned, and homeless pets with loving families.
  • Supporting pet parents with affordable and reliable care solutions.
  • Collaborating with shelters and NGOs to provide meals, medical aid, and other essentials.

What We Do

Pawfect Paradise' primary mission is to find forever loving families and homes for animals in need. We home/rehome orphaned, abandoned, homeless cats and dogs from the streets after providing them with state-of-the-art private medical care to ensure a hassle-free experience for both adopters and pets.

In Addition

  • Educate adopter families on the importance of sterilization.
  • Regularly follow up with adopters to ensure the well-being of the pets adopted from us.
  • Treat injured animals and release them back into their territories. If they cannot survive on their own, we get them adopted.
  • Facilitate rehoming and adoption of animals in areas of low adoption rates.

Over the last year, our team has successfully homed 70+ pups, kittens, and dogs, including several blind, deaf, and handicapped animals. Together, we are making a difference one pet at a time.

Why Adopt?

Adopting a pet not only changes their life but yours too. By choosing adoption, you're saving a life and making room for other animals in need. Plus, you'll gain a loyal friend who will shower you with unconditional love and happiness.

Join us in making a difference! Let's give every stray and homeless pet the chance to find a home, safety, and the love they deserve.
